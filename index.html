About

Pandawa Charcoal

Pandawa Charcoal (PT. Panca Daniswara Mandiri) is an Indonesian company engaged in Manufactures, Suppliers and Exports of Coconut Briquette Charcoal. We produce charcoal of the highest quality, with a production capacity of 4 MT / day for hookah charcoal and barbeque charcoal. Our hookah charcoal and barbeque charcoal are made from natural coconut shells and contain no chemical impurities, and are completely eco-friendly products. Our Mission is to provide high quality of Coconut Briquette Charcoal products for global needs. Thus ensuring and fulfilling environmental and community needs at the same time. Our Vision is to become market leader for coconut briquettes charcoal suppliers for global needs. Your trust and satisfaction is our main goal and motivate us to make our company growth stronger and becoming one of the best leading company for coconut briquettes charcoal.
